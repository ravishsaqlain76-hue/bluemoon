About Blue Moon Residency

Who We Are

Your Trusted Guest House in Islamabad

Blue Moon Residency is a well-established guest house situated in the prestigious F-6/2 sector of Islamabad, Pakistan. We have been providing quality accommodation to travelers, business professionals, and families who seek a comfortable, clean, and affordable place to stay in the heart of the capital.

Our property at House # 2-A, Justice Abdul Rasheed Road offers a peaceful retreat with all modern amenities, while keeping you connected to Islamabad's vibrant city life. Whether you need a short overnight stay or extended accommodation, Blue Moon Residency delivers a home-like experience with professional service.

Our Mission

To provide every guest with a clean, safe, and comfortable stay at an affordable price. We believe in honest hospitality, transparent pricing, and making every visitor feel welcome in Islamabad.

What Sets Us Apart

Prime Location

In F-6/2, minutes from Faisal Mosque, Margalla Hills, and major hospitals.

Modern Rooms

Fully furnished with AC, Wi-Fi, TV, and en-suite bathrooms.

Warm Hospitality

Friendly staff dedicated to making your stay memorable.

Affordable Rates

No upfront payment. Transparent pricing with no hidden charges.
