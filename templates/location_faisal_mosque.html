Stay 5 Minutes from Faisal Mosque at Blue Moon Residency

Blue Moon Residency is the closest quality guest house to Faisal Mosque in Islamabad. Located in F-6/2 — just a 5-minute drive from Pakistan's iconic national mosque — we offer the perfect base for tourists, pilgrims, and anyone visiting the Faisal Mosque area.

Why Stay Near Faisal Mosque?

Faisal Mosque is Pakistan's largest mosque and one of the most visited landmarks in Islamabad. Located at the foot of the Margalla Hills, it attracts thousands of visitors daily. Staying nearby means you can visit at different times of day — including for Fajr prayer or the stunning sunset views — without a long commute.

From Blue Moon Residency, you can also walk to the Margalla Hills Trail 3 entrance (10 min drive), making it an ideal base for combining mosque visits with nature hikes.
